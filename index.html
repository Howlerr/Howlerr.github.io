About Howlerr

Howlerr is a social-media site built to free Creators and Followers from the chains of the existing, limiting, un-free social-media sites. Our platform allows Creators and Followers to express their true identities and ideals without fear of being blocked or silenced. We're a P2P social-media site inspired by the rise of Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, etc. and we welcome contributions from the community.

Howlerr will use a Utility Token called "Paws" to incentivize participants.

Features

  • A platform for Creators and Followers to express their true identities and ideals
  • A P2P social-media site inspired by the rise of Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, etc.
  • An open source project that welcomes contributions from the community
